A Short Summary

My name is Irem Gokce. Irem is given by my sister, Gokce is given by my father. Luckily they had. After studying Computer Engineering at Middle East Technical University and working in Turkey, on the last year of my MSc study in Game Technologies, I moved to Michigan, US, to accompany my spouse. I’ve never felt belonged to or owner of anything. Now, I’m continuing to live by saying “I’m on a long and narrow road, on my way morning and night, I don’t know what state I’m in, I’m on my way morning and night”. Since we are going ahead on this road, then lets leave some roses-rose in here is figurative, it can be books, instruments or art works- behind us so that people coming behind us can get advantage of them, I’ve great and useful ideas and there are subjects to work on to make these thoughts real. The only thing I need is me, it seemed easy saying it in this way.

Continue to create, to sleep at nights with peace of mind
